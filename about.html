About Sam

I write about what technology changes inside a business.

I have spent more than 10 years helping organizations market smarter, generate leads, track results, and turn business data into better decisions. Today, my work is focused on HubSpot strategy and operations, RevOps, CRM systems, and how AI changes the way teams actually work.

The perspective behind the site

I started this site to have a place for the longer version of what I think about business and technology news. A lot of these stories look like market stories at first. Once you work in operations, they start looking like questions about cost, trust, execution, and who has to make the system work.

That is the perspective I bring to the articles. I want the writing to be direct, useful, and connected to real work rather than repeating what a company already said in a press release.
